Experieces

I've started drawing during 2nd grade. People would call me gifted and started following my drawings for tracing. Though it was more of a mere hobby and took drawing breaks from months to years. During 8th grade I started to enjoy drawing again and had a huge spike of imporvement in the 2020 COVID era. From 2023 I have not improved much but discovered a style of art that will forever inspire me. A person named Graeme Borland, creator of an Indie game titled Sort The Court, has his own website dedicated on drawing daily sketches (you can check it out here). To me they were not drawings, they were masterpieces, might be an exaggeration but I digress. It just amazes me on how he can create wonderful world building on just simple sketches which made me want to do the same. Here I am taking landscape references from The Legends of Zelda: Tears of the Kingdom and to drawing from my imagination. I still have ways to go but I want to know where this artistic direction takes me.
